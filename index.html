Project Management Dashboard using PowerBI

Project management dashboards can facilitate communication across all involved members and improve decision-making. In this dashboard, I analyzed the dataset and identified potential risks or issues, and take corrective actions to support the project success.

Product Review Dashboard using Power BI

Product reviews play a crucial role in offering customers insights into product performance, aiding them in making well-informed purchase decisions. This dashboard provides a thorough analysis of customer reviews across various categories, representing products from multiple brands and manufacturers.

Road Accident Analysis using Microsoft Excel

In this project I analysed a Road Accident Dashboard in Microsoft Excel, which serves as a valuable tool for stakeholders such as Ministry of Transport, Traffic Management Agencies, Road Safety Corps and Road Transport Department with valuable insights to make data-driven decisions and implement evidence-based interventions that enhance road safety.

Space Mission Dashboard using Power BI

This dashboard offers a comprehensive view of space missions, enabling stakeholders to assess the performance of space exploration efforts over decades and across different regions.

Energy Consumption Dashboard using Power BI

The project utilizes Power BI interactive visualizations to provide a comprehensive overview of energy usage across various time periods and categories. It helps to monitor consumption patterns, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to optimize eneergy efficiency.

Customer Feedback Analysis using Excel

HR Attrition Analysis using Tableau

This is an Attrition analytics Dashboard built on Tableau . The working of the dashboard is demonstrated below. The dashboard mainly focuses on the attrition of employees while displaying information like the active employee count, job satisfaction of employees depending on the job role, etc. The dashboard includes 5 KPIs and a variety of graphs (bar chart, pie chart, lollipop chart, histogram, and donut chart) and filters for visualizing the required data.

Data Science Jobs Salaries Dashboard using Tableau

LinkedIn Profile Analysis using Power BI
